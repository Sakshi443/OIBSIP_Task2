Biography

" You have to fight to reach
your dream. You have to
sacrifice and work hard for it. "

Born on June 24, 1987, in Rosario, Argentina, Lionel Messi's journey from a young boy dribbling a ball in the streets of his hometown to a global football icon is emblematic of dedication and passion. Overcoming early challenges, including a growth hormone deficiency, Messi's move to Barcelona's La Masia academy marked the genesis of an unparalleled career. With FC Barcelona, he crafted a legacy marked by tenacity, skill, and an insatiable drive, clinching multiple La Liga titles, Champions League triumphs, and individual honors, with a record-breaking six Ballon d'Or awards showcasing his dominance in the sport.

Beyond his myriad achievements, Messi's unwavering commitment to the Argentine national team stands as a testament to his patriotic spirit. Despite navigating the highs and lows of international tournaments, his leadership culminated in Argentina's long-awaited Copa America victory in 2021, cementing his place as a national hero. Off the pitch, Messi's humility, profound dedication to his craft, and philanthropic endeavors, notably through the Leo Messi Foundation, underscore a legacy that transcends football. His unparalleled talent, unwavering passion for the sport, and relentless pursuit of excellence culminated in the 2019 Ballon d'Or, recognizing him as the pinnacle of footballing greatness.

Timeline

  • June 24, 1987: Lionel Andres Messi Cuccittini was born in Rosario, Argentina.
  • March 30, 1994: Messi joined Newell's Old Boys at the age of six and played for this team for about six years. While he was at the youth club, he scored over 500 goals.
  • December 14, 2000: Messi and his family relocated to the city of Barcelona in Spain. The family moved into an apartment that was very close to Barcelona's football team's stadium, Camp Nou.
  • February 2, 2002:Messi became a student at Barcelona's youth academy, La Masia and officially enrolled in the Royal Spanish Football Federation (RFEF).
  • February 4, 2004: Messi signed his first professional contract with Barcelona (valid until 2012) with a buyout clause of €30 million.
  • October 16, 2004: Messi made his first appearance in a match against FC Porto.
  • July 24, 2005: On his 18th birthday, Messi landed his first contract as a senior team player. The contract was completed on June 24, 2005. The contract outlined that he would be an official player with the Barcelona football club until 2010 with a €150 million buyout clause
  • May 17, 2006: Messi achieved Wins his first UEFA Champions League title with Barcelona.
  • August 23, 2008: Messi had the privilege of playing at the Football at the 2008 Summer Olympics which was held in Beijing and a number of other cities in the People's Republic of China. With Messi as a key player, Argentina won the tournament.
  • May 27, 2009: Messi Scores in Barcelona's 2-0 victory over Manchester United in the UEFA Champions League final; receives his first Ballon d'Or in December.
  • December 01, 2009: Lionel Messi was awarded his very first Ballon d'Or.
  • January 10, 2011: Messi received his second consecutive Ballon d'Or
  • January 7, 2013: Messi was awarded his fourth Ballon d'Or
  • January 11, 2016: Messi was awarded his fifth Ballon d'Or
  • December 03, 2019: Messi was awarded his sixth Ballon d'Or
  • September 29, 2020: Lionel Messi was declared the Most Marketable Athlete of 2020. His rival, Cristiano Ronaldo finished at the second spot on the list. The two are on the list ahead of other major athletes like Lebron James and Virat Kohli. 
  • August 10, 2021: After joining Barcelona at age 13 and 35 trophies won, Messi completed a free transfer at French club Paris Saint-Germain, following the expiration of his contract at Barcelona. Messi chose 30 as his squad number, which was the number he wore on his Barcelona debut in 2003.
  • November 29, 2021: On 29 November 2021 Messi won his seventh Ballon d'Or. He helped the Argentina win the 2021 Copa America and he scored 40 goals for club and national team in all competitions.
  • June 01, 2022: Argentina won against Italy with 3-0 in the 2022 Finalissima at Wembley. This final, which pitted the winner of the European championship against the winner of the Copa America, Messi was once again decisive, with two assists and was named player of the match.
  • 18 December 2022: In the 2022 FIFA World Cup final at Lusail Stadium, Messi scored Argentina's opening goal with a penalty. Despite Kylian Mbappé equalizing twice for France, Messi scored again in extra time. With the match tied 3-3, Argentina won 4-2 on penalties, securing their first World Cup in 36 years. Messi, with 7 goals and 3 assists, was awarded the Golden Ball as the tournament's best player.
  • 15 July 2023: Lionel Messi signed for Major League Soccer club Inter Miami after leaving Paris Saint-Germain as a free agent. Messi signed a two-and-a-half seasons contract, with an option to extend for a further year in the 2026 season.
